Libertas Ultra – smart polity. Unified Charitable Foundation

There are many charitable foundations (further - CF) in modern states. However, they are all scattered and even forced to compete with each other. It cannot be called a rare phenomenon that the CF is established primarily for self-promotion in political activities or for other personal purposes

This activity exists, but its money turnover is negligible in comparison with the request for help from those who need it. In addition, charity is not popular in society: the manifestation of social responsibility is negligible compared to its potential

Libertas Ultra promotes the creation of a Unified Charitable Foundation - it will unite the efforts of all funds in the country, eliminate cynical self-promotion, and will be a powerful organization with huge resources that will not bypass anyone in need

Charity needs to be a mandatory tax on the working population. Even if you give $5 a year, this is a enormous amount of money (in case of Ukraine, it is $100 million), which can solve these problems at once: nutrition and care in orphanages, expensive treatment of a group of people, as well as shelter, clothing and food for homeless people and street animals

In addition, casinos can be involved. In some country, I heard for a case that all casinos and bookmakers belong to the state, and the profits from them go to charity. An excellent idea: there are no flaws in it, therefore can be considered as a part of this reform of the "United CF"

Summary: The strong is given strength to help the weak
