Citizens rating (China's case, but better)

China has a political rating of citizens. So, insulting the authorities, the rating goes down and you cannot find a job. The idea of ranking citizens is great, although China's case is terrible

Me and my best friend Andy propose the idea of a charity rating of citizens. Donating to charity funds, the rating will grow

Thus, people with a high rating will be able to use the list of privileges: lower tax rates; the possibility of buying real estate in a normal residential area; priority and fast service in state institutions, etc.
