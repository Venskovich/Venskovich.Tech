Drones as firefighters

If someone does not know, there are such small balls that, when thrown into a fire, explode under the temperature influence and spray powder, which, in turn, extinguishes the fire. Excellent technology and very effective: you can see this by watching a couple of videos

They are called: "autonomous sphere of powder fire extinguishing"

A fire is a disaster that requires an immediate response. 911 employees do it, but call response rate can be accelerated with drones

What if we add a box with 4-6 balls to the drone, which are ready to be thrown into the fire? The fire can probably be extinguished before the 911 arrives

The drone has a huge advantage over the 911 car: it flies in a straight line, bypassing all road traffic and can reach speed up to 90km/h - it is not difficult to estimate that the arrival speed to a call with a drone can be 2 times higher

What remains: someone of observers or who who called 911, must throw the balls into the fire and then wait for the the 911 car arrival

If the field is on fire, drones-bombers could even be developed. These balls will be in their bomb bay. Unlike a drone, the speed of a drone-bomber can reach 300 km/h, so the advantage over cars is obvious

There is another concept: a drone with a cannon. Unlike a drone case with a box, which requires the participation of third parties, a drone with a cannon flies to the window and fires a ball through it. The ball soon extinguishes the fire. Probably, such a cannon mechanism will be single-shot, so a flight crew of several drones can be raised to a call
